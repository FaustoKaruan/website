About DFX.

DFX is a DeFiChain Community Project, which was started in April 2021, with the aim of exchanging Fiat money like EUR or CHF into $DFI, dBTC, dETH or tokenized stocks like dTSLA or dAAPL on DeFiChain intuitively and easily with a standard SEPA bank transfer.

More about our Vision

DFX is the first service provider to offer a service that allows you to invest directly into crypto asstes on the DeFiChain or sell them via normal SEPA bank transfers and Auto-DCA.

The easiest & fastest application to exchange for dBTC, dETH &
$DFI.


Our Vision.

We are helping to achieve maximum freedom by giving everyone responsibility over their own finances, so that no one need to depend on third-party providers such as banks, insurance companies or financial service providers, or on FIAT currencies. The right to self-determination over one's own finances is a fundamental part of every individual's freedom.

Freedom & DeFi.

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) can guarantee and protect this freedom through decentralized financial instruments that are censorship-resistant, expropriation-proof and can be held and traded by everyone individually for an unlimited period of time. DFX was started to provide an intuitive application that allows users to get in or rather out of the DeFi ecosystem quickly and easily.
