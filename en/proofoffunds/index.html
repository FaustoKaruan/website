Proof of Origin of Funds

You've become rich with Crypto and want to exchange a part of your wealth into Fiat? Exchanges and banks require a proof of origin of funds related to larger amounts of funds.

Proof of funds by means of DFX

We're pleased to help and support you with our experts. We can organize a phone call to discuss all relevant aspects in order to document your origin of funds in an uncomplicated and easy way. Furthermore, we're pleased to help with preparing to withdraw your funds to your bank account, so that no issues occur. Please contact us for further information: contact@dfx.swiss .
